How your data is handled.

StyleSnap is built to give you editorial-quality styling advice without holding onto your personal information. This page explains, in plain language, exactly what the app sees, where it goes, and how long it stays.

01Who we are

StyleSnap (the “app”) is an AI-powered personal styling assistant for Android. The app is published under the package name com.stylesnapai.app and is referred to in this policy as “we”, “us”, or “StyleSnap”. By installing or using the app you agree to the practices described below.

02What data we handle

StyleSnap is designed to collect as little as possible. The categories below cover everything the app touches.

Photos and images you choose

  • Outfit photos you capture or select from your gallery for analysis or comparison.
  • Closet entries you save inside the app for future reference.

Information you provide

  • Style preferences (occasion, vibe, body-type hints) entered into the input form.
  • Optional notes or tags you attach to saved looks.

Information generated by the app

  • AI-generated style readouts, scores, and tips produced for your photos.
  • Daily usage counters (number of analyses, ad cooldown timers) used to enforce free-tier limits.

Information collected automatically by advertising partners

  • Advertising identifier (Android Advertising ID), device and network information, and approximate location may be processed by Google AdMob to serve and measure ads. See section 05 for details and opt-out.

What we do not collect: we do not ask for your name, email, phone number, contacts, calendar, precise GPS location, microphone, or browsing history. The app has no user account system.

03How your photos are used

When you ask StyleSnap to analyze an outfit, the selected image is sent over an encrypted HTTPS connection to our styling server, which runs an open-source vision-language model (Ollama / LLaVA) on infrastructure we operate. The server processes the image in memory only to generate the style readout you see in the app, then returns the result to your device.

  • Images are not used to train any AI model.
  • Images are not shared with advertisers, brokers, or third parties.
  • Images are not retained on the server after the response is sent; only short-lived, rotating diagnostic logs (HTTP status codes, latency, error messages — no image content) may exist for troubleshooting and are purged automatically.

If you choose to save an analyzed look to your closet, the photo and its style readout are stored locally on your device only — they are not uploaded anywhere else.

04Stored on your device

The following items live in the app's private storage on your phone and never leave it unless you explicitly export them:

  • Your closet entries (photos + AI readouts).
  • Your daily-tip preference and last-shown date.
  • Today's analysis credits and ad cooldown timer.
  • Theme preference (light / dark) and other UI settings.

You can clear all of this at any time by uninstalling the app or by clearing app data from Android Settings.

05Advertising & AdMob

StyleSnap is free to use and is supported by ads served through Google AdMob. Ads are shown between analyses, with a daily cap and cooldown to keep the experience light. The first analysis of each session is always ad-free.

AdMob may collect and process the following to deliver and measure ads:

  • Android Advertising ID (a resettable identifier).
  • Device information (model, OS version, language, screen size).
  • General network information (IP-based coarse location, mobile carrier).
  • Ad interaction events (impressions, clicks, completions).

AdMob's processing is governed by Google's privacy policies. To learn more or to opt out of personalized ads:

  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services
  • Google Privacy Policy
  • On Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads to reset or delete your Advertising ID and opt out of personalized ads.

06Third-party services

StyleSnap relies on the following third parties. Each is used only for the purpose described.

  • Google AdMob — in-app advertising. See section 05.
  • Google Play Services — required Android infrastructure (Play Integrity, ads SDK, crash reporting via Play).
  • Google Fonts — web fonts served on this privacy page (not used inside the app itself).

We do not sell your data to any third party, and we do not engage in cross-app or cross-site tracking outside the AdMob integration described above.

07Retention & deletion

  • Photos sent for analysis — held in server memory only for the duration of the request, then discarded.
  • Diagnostic server logs — rotated and deleted automatically within 14 days; never contain image content.
  • On-device data (closet, settings, counters) — kept until you clear app data or uninstall the app.
  • AdMob data — retained per Google's policies; you can reset your Advertising ID at any time from Android Settings.

Because StyleSnap has no account system, deleting the app from your device is sufficient to remove all data we hold about you on that device.

08Children's privacy

StyleSnap is intended for users 13 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the app, please contact us (section 12) and we will take reasonable steps to delete any associated data.

09Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under laws such as the GDPR (EU/UK), CCPA / CPRA (California), or DPDP Act (India), including the right to access, correct, or delete data we hold about you, and to object to certain processing.

  • Access & deletion of on-device data — available directly in the app or by clearing app data in Android Settings.
  • Personalised ads — you may opt out via Settings → Privacy → Ads on Android.
  • Other requests — email us at the address in section 12 and we will respond within 30 days.

10Security

We use industry-standard practices to protect data in transit and at rest:

  • HTTPS/TLS for every network request between the app and our styling server.
  • Sandboxed app storage on Android, so on-device data is isolated from other apps.
  • Code obfuscation and integrity checks (R8 minification, Play Integrity) on release builds.

No system is perfectly secure. If you suspect a vulnerability, please report it via the contact details below so we can investigate promptly.

11Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect new features, legal requirements, or operational changes. The “Last updated” date at the top of the page will always show the most recent revision. Material changes will be highlighted in the app on first launch after the change takes effect.
